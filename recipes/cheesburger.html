Cheese burger

Everyone's favorite Cheese burger

Available at every fastfood outlet and a joy to eat.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef patty
  • Bun
  • Cheese slice
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  • Cut the bun into two pieces from the middle.
  • Place the patty on the lower part of the bun
  • Place the cheese slice on the patty
  • Apply ketchup on the top bun and assemble the burger.
