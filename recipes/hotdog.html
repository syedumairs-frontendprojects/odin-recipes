Hot dog

Everyone's favorite Hot dog

Available at every block in New york and a joy to eat.

Ingredients

  • Boiled Sasuage
  • Hotdog Bun
  • Mustard
  • Ketchup

Instructions

  • Cut the bun vertically into two pieces.
  • Place the patty inn the center of the bun
  • Apply ketchup and mustard on top of the Sasuage and enjoy the Hotdog.
